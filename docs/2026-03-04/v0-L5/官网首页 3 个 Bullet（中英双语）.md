## 官网首页 3 个 Bullet（中英双语）

1. **把 AI 生成变成可裁决的工业流程**
   从一句话需求到完整交付，全程通过 Gate 裁决、证据链（EvidenceRef）与 Permit 放行。
   **Turn AI output into a decisionable production pipeline**
   From one-line intent to full deliverables, enforced by Gates, EvidenceRefs, and Permit-based release control.

2. **沉淀可复用的治理资产，越用越强**
   规则库、合同/蓝图规范、黄金样例与审计包持续版本化沉淀，迭代可回溯、可复现。
   **Build reusable governance assets that compound over time**
   Versioned rules, contracts/blueprints, golden fixtures, and audit packs—iterable, traceable, reproducible.

3. **为团队提供可验证的放行标准**
   通过审计才签发 Permit；发布链路验证 Permit，避免绕过与“靠感觉上线”。
   **Give teams verifiable release standards**
   Permit is issued only after audit; your release chain validates it—no bypass, no “ship by vibes.”

## 风险声明（避免过度承诺）

**风险声明（中文）**
GM-SkillForge 旨在降低不确定性并提升可审计性，但无法保证覆盖所有安全风险或业务逻辑风险。审计结果依赖于输入需求、配置策略、规则版本与执行环境的完整性与一致性；对外部系统（第三方 API、网络环境、权限配置等）的实际行为不作绝对保证。建议在关键业务场景中保留人工审核与分级发布策略，并根据组织要求持续更新策略与规则。

**Risk Disclaimer (English)**
GM-SkillForge is designed to reduce uncertainty and improve auditability, but it does not guarantee coverage of all security or business-logic risks. Audit outcomes depend on the completeness and consistency of inputs, policies, rule versions, and execution environments; behaviors of external systems (third-party APIs, networks, credentials) are not guaranteed. For critical workloads, keep human review and staged releases in place, and continuously update policies and rules to match your organization’s requirements.
