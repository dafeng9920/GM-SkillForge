### 3. Signal Generation Process
```
Input: MarketData (OHLCV)
Process:
  1. Calculate Fast MA (period=10) and Slow MA (period=30)
  2. Calculate MA Spread = (Fast - Slow) / Slow
  3. Calculate Trend Strength = MA Spread 5-period rolling mean
  4. Calculate ATR (period=14)
  5. Calculate Momentum = 5-period price change
  6. Calculate Volume Ratio = Volume / 20-period MA
  7. Generate Buy Signal when all conditions met
  8. Generate Sell Signal when all conditions met
  9. Calculate dynamic Stop Loss and Take Profit based on ATR
Output: Signal (LONG/SHORT/FLAT) + Confidence + Stop Loss + Take Profit
```
